About Me

I am a 15-year-old photographer and designer driven by the energy and discipline of competitive sports. As a three-sport athlete competing in football, baseball, and wrestling, I spend most of my time on the field or on the mat. This firsthand experience as an athlete gives me a unique lens into the world of sports media; I don't just see the action, I understand the grit and strategy behind every play.

Whether I am capturing the intensity of a pin in wrestling, the precision of a baseball swing, or the high-stakes atmosphere of a Friday night football game, my goal is to translate that athletic passion into striking visual stories. My design work complements this by bringing a clean, modern aesthetic to the raw power of sports. I am constantly looking to merge my life on the team with my creative projects to push the boundaries of what sports photography can be.
